१. आफू बस्ने सहरको Rathaus या Ausländerbehörde मा गएर Verpflichtungserklärung बनाउने । त्यसको लागि तपाईंको पछिल्ला ३ महिनाको salary sheet र passport लगेर जानुपर्ने हुन्छ । साथै बोलाउन लागेको मान्छेको पासपोर्ट नम्बर पनि फाराममा भर्नुपर्ने हुन्छ । 

२. Verpflichtungserklärung को सक्कल कपि नेपाल पठाउने । प्राय नेपाल जानेले कागजपत्र लगिदिन्छन् । त्यो नै सजिलो र पक्का उपाय हुन्छ ।

३. जर्मन एम्बेसीको वेबसाइटमा प्रत्येक भिसाका लागि चाहिने कागजपत्रको सूची हुन्छ । त्यसै अनुसार अन्य कागजात तयार गर्ने । ९० दिन सम्मको भिसाका लागि चाहिंने आवश्यक कागजातको सूची यसमा हेर्न सकिन्छः

Steps to invite parents/friends from Nepal

	1.	You have to issue sponsor letter (Verpflichtungserklärung) from City office (Rathaus) or foreigners office (Ausländerbehörde). You can google Verpflichtungserklärung and your city name to know where to get it. You have to take 3 months salary sheet and you Passport along with you. The form also requires passport number of the person you are inviting. 
	2.	The original copy of sponsor letter (Verpflichtungserklärung) to Nepal. Normally, they can be sent along with people flying to Nepal. Its is more safe and certain way as even DHL looses documents or delays them. 
	3.	The check-list of ducments required for visa are available in German embassy website. The requirement is different according to visa category. Here, you can find a check-list for 90 days visitor visa: 

         https://kathmandu.diplo.de/np-en/service/01-VisaEinreise/-/1672492

	4.	You can find a link to an online application for visa in same page above.  https://videx.diplo.de/videx/desktop/index.html#start
	5.	Once the document are ready, you can take appoint for visa. Depending on visa categories there are different links for appoibtment. You have to choose “Schengen Visa – Vaccinated with Covishield, AstraZeneca, Moderna or Biontech” for 90 days visa from the link below. https://service2.diplo.de/rktermin/extern/choose_realmList.do?locationCode=kath&request_locale=en
